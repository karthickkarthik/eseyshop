Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Fill in your shipping and payment details, then confirm your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, UPI payments, and cash on delivery.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days, while express shipping takes 1-2 business days. Free shipping is available on orders over $50.

Can I return or exchange items?

Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Contact our support team to initiate a return.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the Orders section.
